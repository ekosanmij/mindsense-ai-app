    FAQ and Safety

    Key trust and product questions

    Is MindSense medical care?

    MindSense provides wellness support and does not replace emergency services or licensed clinical care.

    How does confidence work?

    Confidence reflects data recency and coverage. The app explains confidence so recommendations are interpreted appropriately.

    Where is data stored?

    Core runtime behavior is local-first. The app is designed so core guidance remains available without cloud dependency.

    What if someone needs urgent help?

    Urgent safety concerns should use immediate crisis resources. In the US, call or text 988 for crisis support.
