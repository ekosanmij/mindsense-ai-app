    FAQ

    Trust, safety, and implementation boundaries

    Is MindSense a medical device?

    No. MindSense is a wellness support app and does not replace emergency services or licensed clinical care.

    Where is user data stored in the current build?

    Core runtime behavior is local-first and persisted on device. Cloud sync is not part of the current implementation.

    How are recommendation confidence levels handled?

    Confidence is surfaced with labels and percentages tied to coverage/recency so users and teams can interpret guidance quality.

    What happens for urgent safety needs?

    Immediate risk should use emergency channels. In the United States, call or text 988 for crisis support.
