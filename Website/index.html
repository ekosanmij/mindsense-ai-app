    FAQ

    Common diligence and adoption questions

    Is MindSense a medical product?

    No. MindSense is a wellness app and does not replace emergency or licensed clinical care.

    How is data handled in the current build?

    Core runtime behavior is local-first on device. Cloud sync is not part of v1.0.0.

    How do recommendations communicate confidence?

    Confidence labels and coverage framing are exposed to support interpretation quality.

    What should users do in urgent crises?

    Use local emergency channels. In the US, call or text 988 for crisis support.
