MindSense terms summary

MindSense AI provides wellness-focused guidance and self-regulation support. It is not emergency care and should not be used as a substitute for licensed clinical treatment when urgent risk is present.

Usage boundaries

  • Use guidance as supportive wellness direction, not medical diagnosis.
  • For urgent safety concerns, use local emergency channels immediately.
  • In the US, call or text 988 for crisis support.

Questions and updates

For terms clarifications or partnership review requests, contact partnerships@mindsense.ai.
