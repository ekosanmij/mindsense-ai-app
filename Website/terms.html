MindSense terms summary

MindSense AI provides wellness-oriented behavioral guidance. It is not intended as emergency care or a diagnostic medical product.

Usage boundaries

  • Use MindSense as wellness support and not as a substitute for licensed clinical care.
  • For urgent or immediate safety concerns, contact local emergency services.
  • In the United States, call or text 988 for crisis support.
