MindSense terms summary

MindSense AI provides wellness-oriented guidance and behavior support. It is not intended as emergency or diagnostic medical care.

Usage boundaries

  • Use MindSense as wellness support, not as a substitute for licensed clinical care.
  • For urgent safety concerns, use local emergency services immediately.
  • In the United States, call or text 988 for crisis support.

Product status notes
