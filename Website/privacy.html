Privacy

MindSense privacy posture summary

MindSense AI is implemented as a local-first iOS app for its core runtime behavior in v1.0.0. This page summarizes implementation posture and does not replace legal advice.

Current implementation posture

  • Core product loop state is persisted on-device using local storage.
  • No cloud sync pipeline is implemented in this build.
  • Auth uses Sign in with Apple and local session persistence.
  • Confidence and data-coverage indicators are shown in-product to support interpretation quality.

User controls

  • Settings includes privacy/data related entry points and policy access.
  • Health diagnostics and source controls are surfaced in-app.
  • Users can sign out and reset core local state from account controls.

Safety and support boundaries

  • MindSense is framed as wellness support and not emergency care.
  • Crisis support direction is explicit, including US 988 guidance.
  • For urgent concerns, use local emergency channels immediately.

Contact

For privacy or data-handling questions, contact support@mindsense.ai.
