Privacy

MindSense privacy posture summary

MindSense AI is currently built as a local-first iOS application for its core runtime behavior. This page summarizes implementation posture, not legal advice.

Current implementation posture

  • Core loop behavior runs locally in the app and persists on device.
  • Guidance is presented with confidence and coverage framing.
  • The product is framed as wellness support, not emergency care.

Data and controls

  • Settings includes privacy/data control entry points and policy links.
  • Health and signal preferences are managed from in-app settings surfaces.
  • Safety pathways include crisis support guidance where relevant.

Contact

For privacy or data-handling questions, contact support@mindsense.ai.
