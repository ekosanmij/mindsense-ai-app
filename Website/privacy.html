Privacy

MindSense privacy posture

MindSense AI is designed as a local-first iOS experience for core daily runtime behavior. Product messaging and interaction flows are framed around confidence and data quality context so users can interpret guidance appropriately.

Data handling posture

  • Core user state, sessions, and app behavior are stored locally by default.
  • Guidance copy is confidence-framed and avoids unsupported certainty claims.
  • Safety messaging is explicit about non-emergency boundaries.

Support and policy requests

For full policy requests, data handling questions, or support, contact support@mindsense.ai.

This summary describes current implementation posture and is not legal advice.
