Privacy

MindSense privacy summary (as-built v1.0.0)

This page summarizes current implementation posture from source. It is informational and does not replace legal advice.

1. Data storage posture

  • Core app state is local-first and persisted on-device with `UserDefaults` and Codable models.
  • No cloud sync pipeline is implemented in this build.
  • Analytics events are stored locally as bounded event records.

2. Authentication posture

  • Sign in with Apple is the implemented authentication path.
  • Session identifiers and account display metadata are persisted locally for return routing.
  • No backend identity sync layer is implemented in this build.

3. Health and signal posture

  • Apple Health diagnostics surfaces are available in Settings for permission status and quality context.
  • The current build does not ship a full real HealthKit ingestion pipeline.
  • Users can resync, rebuild baseline, or delete health-derived data from in-app controls.

4. User controls

  • Settings changes autosave and can be updated anytime (appearance, motion, notifications, signal preferences).
  • Sign out clears session and resets core local seeded state.
  • Privacy and safety pathways are accessible from Settings.

5. Safety boundary

  • MindSense is a wellness support product and not emergency care.
  • For urgent concerns, use local emergency channels immediately.
  • In the US, call or text 988 for crisis support.

6. Contact

For privacy or data-handling questions, contact support@mindsense.ai.
